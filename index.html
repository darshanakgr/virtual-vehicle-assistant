One of the major issues in a vehicle sale is that the number of sales persons with a sound knowledge about each vehicle's specifications are low. Usually there are about one or two persons. When few customers arrive at the same time, it becomes a major concern. What we are expecting through our project is to minimize the workload of sales persons and to increase profit by satisfying customers.
